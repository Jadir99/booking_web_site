City tour Agadir

Explore Agadir’s Culture, History, and Stunning Views

Join us on a fascinating city tour of Agadir, a modern coastal gem with rich history, breathtaking views, and vibrant culture. From ancient ruins to bustling souks, this tour offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Tour Highlights

  • Agadir Oufella 🏰 – Enjoy panoramic views from the ancient fortress overlooking the city and ocean.
  • Agadir Marina 🚤 – Walk along the luxurious marina, home to stylish cafés and yachts.
  • Mohamed V Mosque 🕌 – Admire the architectural beauty of Agadir’s grand mosque.
  • Souk El Had 🛍️ – Experience the lively atmosphere of Agadir’s biggest market, filled with spices, handicrafts, and local treasures.
  • Argan Oil Cooperative 🌿 – Learn about the traditional production of Morocco’s famous Argan oil.
  • Beautiful Corniche & Beachfront 🌊 – Relax along the scenic coastline and soak in the laid-back beach vibe.

  • ✅ Professional guide to share local insights 🗣️
  • ✅ Stops at key landmarks for sightseeing and photos 📸
