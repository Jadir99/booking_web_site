City tour Agadir

Explore Agadirs Culture, History, and Stunning Views

Join us on a fascinating city tour of Agadir, a modern coastal gem with rich history, breathtaking views, and vibrant culture. From ancient ruins to bustling souks, this tour offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Tour Highlights

  • Agadir Oufella Enjoy panoramic views from the ancient fortress overlooking the city and ocean.
  • Agadir Marina Walk along the luxurious marina, home to stylish cafs and yachts.
  • Mohamed V Mosque Admire the architectural beauty of Agadirs grand mosque.
  • Souk El Had Experience the lively atmosphere of Agadirs biggest market, filled with spices, handicrafts, and local treasures.
  • Argan Oil Cooperative Learn about the traditional production of Moroccos famous Argan oil.
  • Beautiful Corniche & Beachfront Relax along the scenic coastline and soak in the laid-back beach vibe.

What's Included?

  • Hotel pickup & drop-off
  • Professional guide to share local insights
  • Stops at key landmarks for sightseeing and photos
